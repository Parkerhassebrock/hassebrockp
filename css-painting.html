Inspiration

What you are seeing is replication of Bridget Riley's piece KA 2 made entirely with css code. This piece is part of a series of paintings using Egyptian color palletes. Made in 1980 this piece is completely made with vertical lines. While this piece was created using Egyptian colors as inspiration, Riley did not copy the hues she found in her expidition. Instead she decided to replicate the colors from memory, thereby giving the piece a more personal touch touch to reflect her experience.
